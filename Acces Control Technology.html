Access Control Technology

We provide a range of access control systems with technical capabilities from motion sensors to attendance management systems and alarms.

Motion Sensors

High-quality sensors for detecting movement in restricted areas, ideal for enhancing security in offices, warehouses, and homes.

Time Attendance Management System

Advanced real-time data system for collecting and managing employee attendance. Features include:

  • Attendance summary and monthly hours
  • Late arrival and overtime reports
  • Individual leave, attendance, and shift roster reports
  • Real-time monitoring and device management
Burglar Alarms

Reliable alarm systems with real-time alerts and centralized monitoring to protect homes and commercial premises.

  • Automatic data synchronization
  • Role-based access control
  • Email integration and online data storage
Electric Fencing

High-voltage fencing solutions for property protection and perimeter security.

Garage and Gate Opener

Automated gate and garage opening systems with secure access and remote control features.

Magnetic Door Locks

Strong magnetic locks for secure doors, easily integrated with access control and attendance systems.
