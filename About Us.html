Company Overview

Electrum Portlinks Engineering is a general construction and electrical engineering company incorporated in August 2023. E.P Links aims to aspire towards responsible business and high standards of mining and engineering services. We strive to provide innovative, creative and technological cost-effective methods and solutions that satisfy the needs of our customers whilst providing continuous development and benefit of our employees.

E.P Links is a growing global resource service provider, delivering business solutions to its customers. We deliver one of the industry's broadest portfolios and business process outsourcing services and products. We supply a wide range of products and services to various industrial sectors such as Mining and Transportation. E.P Links is a 100% Botswana Citizen-owned company; it was incorporated on 20th May 2016 under the company act chapter 42.01 of the Laws of Republic of Botswana.

Since its formation, E.P Links enjoys a diverse and talented workforce which is firmly committed to the customer and company’s success for now and beyond. We specialize in a combined set of engineering disciplines which include Mechanical, Civil, Electrical and Construction works, that we will discuss further on.

We are a full-service provider with world-class program management and risk management expertise. Our infrastructure advisory team has worked across industries providing professional consultancy services to clients.

We are committed to the sustainability of the communities, our clients’ businesses and the environments in which we live and work.

We evaluate and identify mitigation of environmental and community risks, advising our clients on how to make sustainable business improvements, helping them to manage their reputation when it comes to the environment. Our teams of experts operate at both a local and regional level, enabling us to have an understanding of the implications of the East Africa region’s approaches to planning and the associated legislative requirements.
