Director's Statement

After graduating from Ton Duc Thang University, major in Safety Engineering, Van started his career as a Safety Engineer. From 2011 to 2019, Van worked for GS E&C Corporation and Takenaka Vietnam as Assistant HSE Manager, Functional HSE Manager and HSE Manager. In 2020, Van joined ReeCons Engineering as HSE Manager to develop the QHSE Department. With more than 13 years of experience, he has good knowledge of setting up HSE Management Systems, and imparting knowledge to HSE Engineers so that they can control HSE on site successfully.

“Having gained 25 years of experience in the mining environment, at the most respected mining companies such as Debswana Diamond Company (Botswana), Redwing Gold Mine (Lonrho), Mhangura Copper Mine (Zimbabwe), B.H.P World Minerals (Hartley Platinum Mine Zimbabwe - Australian), I decided to venture out and deliver value to clients through an array of engineering, mining and management aspects. E.P Links aspires towards responsible business and high standards of engineering.”
