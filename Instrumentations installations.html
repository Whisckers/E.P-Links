Instrumentation Services

We provide instrumentation services including installation, calibration, and commissioning of PLCs, SCADA systems, DCS, sensors, transmitters, control valves, and automation components. Our technicians are trained to handle SIEMENS, Mitsubishi, Allen Bradley, and Schneider products, ensuring precise and reliable solutions for industrial applications.

Multi-Carrier System (MCS)

Its configurable linear transport rail can be integrated into existing conveyor systems, with precise synchronization and seamless infeed/outfeed of transport carriers. It adapts to various product formats, sizes, and batch requirements.

  • Installation of Transmitters, Switches & Gauges for Pressure, Temperature, Flow, and Level.
  • Installation of DCS, PLC, SCADA, hazardous area control systems, fire & gas systems.
  • Cabling, marshalling panels, instrument tubing, and impulse piping works.
  • Shutdown maintenance, installation, and commissioning.
  • Testing, calibration & loop checking with certified equipment.
